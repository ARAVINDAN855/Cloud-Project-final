GenAI Use Case Document: Multilingual Translation Platform

Overview:
This document outlines the core generative AI use cases integrated into our serverless Cloud-Based Multilingual Translation Platform.

Use Case 1: 
Context-Aware Idiom and Slang Translation
The Problem: Traditional, rule-based translation APIs often fail when translating cultural idioms, local slang, or context-heavy sentences (e.g., translating "it's raining cats and dogs" literally into another language makes no sense).

The GenAI Solution: By passing the extracted text through a Large Language Model (LLM), the system analyzes the context of the entire paragraph before translating. It identifies idioms and generates the most natural, culturally appropriate equivalent in the target language.

Business Impact: Provides highly accurate, human-like translations that improve user trust and readability compared to standard machine translation.

Use Case 2: 
AI-Driven OCR Error Correction (Hallucination Recovery)
The Problem: When users upload blurry images, handwritten notes, or low-quality documents, standard Optical Character Recognition (OCR) often misreads letters (e.g., reading "rn" as "m", or "5" as "S"). If the source text is wrong, the translation will be completely broken.

The GenAI Solution: Before the translation step, GenAI is used as an intelligent proofreader. It takes the messy, broken text generated by the OCR, looks at the surrounding words, and logically infers what the damaged words were supposed to be, reconstructing the original sentence perfectly.

Business Impact: Drastically increases the reliability of the platform for real-world, low-quality image uploads, preventing cascade errors in translation.

Use Case 3: 
Automated Document Summarization
The Problem: Users frequently upload long documents or articles where they don't need a word-for-word translation, but rather a quick understanding of what the text is about.

The GenAI Solution: We utilize GenAI to ingest the translated output and generate a concise, bulleted summary of the core concepts in the user's preferred language.

Business Impact: Saves processing time, reduces reading time for the end-user, and adds a premium "smart" feature that goes beyond a standard 1-to-1 translator.
